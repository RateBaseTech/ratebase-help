Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

RateBase (“we”, “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what we collect and how we use it.

Information we collect

  • Account: email address and (optionally) a display name when you sign up or use Sign in with Apple or Google.
  • Camera: used only to scan barcodes. Images are not stored or transmitted.
  • Location: used only when you open the Map to find stores near you. It is not stored on our servers.
  • Photos: when you choose a profile picture or attach a photo to a review, only the photos you pick are uploaded.
  • Content you create: reviews, favourites and products you add are stored in your account.

How we use your information

  • To authenticate your account and sync your data across devices.
  • To show your reviews and favourites.
  • We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties, and we do not use it for advertising profiling.

Third-party services

  • Supabase — authentication and encrypted data storage.
  • Apple & Google Sign-In — optional login.
  • Open Food Facts / Open Beauty Facts / Open Products Facts, UPC Item DB, Icecat, Datakick — public product data.
  • Amazon, Walmart, Best Buy, Costco — price links (some are affiliate links).

Data retention & deletion

You can use the app without an account. If you create one, you can delete your account and all associated data at any time from Profile → Delete account, or by emailing ratebase@outlook.com.

Children

RateBase is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes & contact

We may update this policy from time to time. Questions? Contact us at ratebase@outlook.com.
