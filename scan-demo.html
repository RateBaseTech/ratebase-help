Scan Demo — for App Review

RateBase scans product barcodes (EAN/UPC) to show reviews and prices. It does not use QR codes or AR markers. You can test the feature two ways:

Option 1 — Type it (no camera needed)

On the Home screen, type this barcode number in the search bar and press Search:

3017620422003

→ returns the product “Nutella (Ferrero)”. You can also type product names such as Nutella, Coca-Cola or Dove.

Option 2 — Scan it with the camera

Tap the camera button next to the search bar, then point the device camera at this barcode (displayed on another screen or printed):

→ opens the product page for “Nutella”, with the Overview / Prices / Reviews tabs.

Notes

  • The app also works fully without an account (“Continue without account”).
  • A demo account is provided in the App Review Information notes for account features (favourites, reviews).
