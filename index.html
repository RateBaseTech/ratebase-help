Frequently asked questions

How do I scan a product?

Tap the camera button next to the search bar on the Home screen, then point your camera at a product barcode. You can also type a product name or barcode in the search bar.

How do I create an account?

On the login screen, sign up with your email, or use Sign in with Apple or Sign in with Google. You can also browse without an account.

How do I save favourites or write a review?

Tap the bookmark icon on any product to save it to Favourites. Open a product and tap “Write a review” to share your opinion. These features require a free account.

How do I delete my account and data?

Go to Profile → Delete account. This permanently removes your account, reviews, favourites and profile. You can also email us at ratebase@outlook.com to request deletion.

Are prices and links accurate?

Prices and availability come from public product databases and may vary by store and over time. Some links are affiliate links (you pay the same price; RateBase may earn a small commission that helps keep the app free).

The map can’t find stores near me

Make sure location access is allowed for RateBase in iOS Settings, then tap the store-count badge to retry. Store data comes from OpenStreetMap and coverage varies by area.
